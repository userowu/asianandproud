asia is more than korea, china, japan.

Mainstream media often portrays Asian people as those

only with East Asian descent. How often have you been exposed

to only Korean, Japanese, or Chinese representation as the only "Asia?"

Not to mention, Hollywood East Asian representation is still rare and

mediocre at best. The poor representation of South and Southeast Asians are

constantly overlooked, and not necessarily due to East Asian representation.

It is the fault of millionaire studios, producers, writers, directors who

will not spare a second to write stories of these major ethnic groups.

not your model minority

Hey! My name is Ling. I am a senior high school student in

the Bay Area. I currently am taking 6 AP classes, and I

have a 4.0 GPA throughout all my years at school. Math and science

are my favorite subjects. I play piano every weekend, and I

tutor my peers in calculus in my free time. I am not very good

at driving either. How long did you continue reading until you

realized that I was lying about all of that?


The model minority myth is a stereotype that is often used

to categorize Asian people. Phrases such as "Wow! You are good

at math, right? Because you are Asian!" or "Asians are super

smart!" are examples of microaggressions used against Asian people.

These comments are commonly used in situations ignorantly

to either "compliment" and diminish an Asian person's accomplishments.

There is no such thing as a good stereotype because all stereotypes

limit and box individuals into characterizations that do not define

their actual person. By validating stereotypes, you see the person

through a prejudiced lens. And many Asians suffer under the pressure of

trying to meet those unwarranted expectations when in reality, Asians

are ordinary humans who want to be respected

and seen as their own individual.
